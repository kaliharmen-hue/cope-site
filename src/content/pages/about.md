## About the studio

COPE is a bold, art-led brand creating original paintings and custom murals for people who want more personality, colour, and individuality in their spaces.

Keith creates graffiti- and modern-inspired artwork shaped by strong line, bold colour, expressive energy, and a clear instinct for work that stands out rather than blends in. The paintings are designed to feel lively, characterful, and full of presence, whether they live in a home, studio, workspace, or later expand into apparel and other formats.

At launch, COPE is focused on a clear core offer:

- original artwork
- murals
- custom commissions

Over time, that will expand carefully into apparel and selected creative projects where the fit is right.

The goal is simple: build an art brand rooted in originality, strong visual identity, and work that feels made by real people rather than mass produced.

COPE is led by Keith's painting practice and shaped by Kali's focus on brand, launch, customer experience, and building the business side with care from day one.
