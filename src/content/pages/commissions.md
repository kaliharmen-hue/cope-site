## Bespoke artwork

Selected commissions are available for people looking for an original piece created around a specific idea, space, or brief.

Whether the work is for a home, a gift, a personal collection, or a creative environment, the aim is to create something with real presence rather than a one-size-fits-all result.

Commissions can be shaped around:

- colour palette
- subject or mood
- size and format
- where the work will live
- the kind of visual energy you want the piece to carry

Each commission begins with a conversation so the brief can be understood properly before any direction is set.

The process usually includes:

1. Initial enquiry
2. Brief and direction
3. Quote and timeline
4. Creation and delivery

If you are enquiring about a commission, it helps to include any useful details such as intended size, location, preferred colours, and whether the piece is for a personal or professional setting.
