This is the first Journal entry for the studio, and a starting point for documenting the work as it develops.

The business is being built around a focused core:

- original artwork
- murals
- custom commissions

That gives the brand a strong foundation without trying to do too much too early.

The visual direction comes from bold street-art influence, expressive colour, and artwork that is meant to feel alive rather than over-polished. The goal is to create work with identity, whether that takes the form of a framed piece, a mural concept, or a bespoke commission.

This first stage of the launch is intentionally lean. Instead of waiting until everything is fully formed, the focus is on building the site, putting original work in front of people, and creating a strong base that can grow properly over time.

That means the early stages will include:

- original framed works for sale
- mural development
- commission enquiries
- process and behind-the-scenes updates

Over time, the plan is to expand into apparel and selected creative projects where the fit is right, but the most important thing now is clarity.

This Journal will be used to share new work, ideas in progress, launch decisions, mural development, and the reality of building a creative business from the ground up.
